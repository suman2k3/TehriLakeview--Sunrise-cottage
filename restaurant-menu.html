About our Restaurant

Experience delightful dining at Tehri Lake View Sunrise Cottages, where great food meets breathtaking surroundings. Our restaurant serves a variety of Indian, Chinese, and local Uttarakhand dishes prepared with fresh ingredients and authentic flavors.

AUTHENTIC DINING EXPERIENCE

At Tehri Lake View Sunrise Cottages, our restaurant offers a delightful culinary experience with a variety of Indian, Chinese, and local Uttarakhand dishes. Prepared using fresh ingredients and traditional recipes, every meal is crafted to satisfy your taste and complement your stay amidst the scenic beauty of Tehri Lake.

Whether you're starting your day with a hearty breakfast, enjoying a family lunch, or ending the evening with a delicious dinner, our warm hospitality and inviting ambiance ensure a memorable dining experience for every guest. We take pride in serving food that brings comfort, flavor, and happiness to your table.

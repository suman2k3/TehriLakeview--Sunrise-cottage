Hotel FAQ

Clear answers for room booking, arrival, food, amenities, and travel planning at Tehri Lake View Sunrise Cottages.

What are the check-in and check-out timings?

Standard hotel check-in and check-out timings may vary by booking date and room availability. Please confirm your arrival time while booking so the team can prepare your room and guide you smoothly.

What room types are available?

Guests can explore comfortable Deluxe Rooms, Family Rooms, and Lake View Rooms. The rooms are designed for couples, families, and small groups who want a peaceful stay near Tehri Lake.

Is Wi-Fi available at the property?

Yes. Public property information lists Wi-Fi among the available amenities. Network speed can depend on weather and hill-area connectivity, so guests with urgent work should keep a backup data option.

Does the hotel have a restaurant?

Yes. Tehri Lake View Sunrise Cottages has restaurant and dining support, with breakfast, tea, snacks, Indian meals, and group meal options available. You can also view the restaurant menu from the website.

Is parking available?

Parking is listed among the property amenities. Since hill-road access and seasonal demand can affect space, it is best to share your vehicle details before arrival.

Where is the property located?

The property address is Kutta Zakh Road, Tehri, Uttarakhand 249001. The location is suited for guests planning Tehri Lake, Tehri Dam, boating areas, and nearby mountain drives.

What can guests do around Tehri Lake?

Tehri Lake is known for scenic views and adventure activities such as boating and water sports when local operators and weather conditions permit. Guests can also visit Tehri Dam, viewpoints, temples, Kanatal, and Dhanaulti.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellation rules depend on how you booked your stay. For direct bookings, contact the hotel team. For bookings made through a travel platform, follow the policy shown in your booking confirmation.

What should I carry for check-in?

Please carry a valid government-issued photo ID for each adult guest. If you booked online, keep your booking confirmation handy for faster check-in.

Is the stay suitable for families?

Yes. The property is a calm choice for families looking for comfortable rooms, lake views, restaurant support, and easy access to Tehri sightseeing.

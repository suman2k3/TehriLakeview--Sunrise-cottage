Best Places to Visit Around Tehri Lake

Tehri Lake is one of the most memorable places to explore in Uttarakhand because it brings mountain views, blue water, fresh air, and adventure activities together in one calm destination. The lake was formed around the Tehri Dam area, where old Tehri was submerged and the population shifted toward New Tehri. Today, the district tourism information highlights Tehri Lake as a major adventure tourism destination with activities such as jet skiing, boating, and hot air balloon experiences.

Guests staying at Tehri Lake View Sunrise Cottages can plan an easy day around Tehri Lake, Tehri Dam, Koti Colony, local viewpoints, and scenic drives toward Kanatal, Dhanaulti, Surkanda Devi Temple, and New Tehri town. Start early for soft sunrise light over the water, keep the afternoon for boating or relaxed sightseeing, and return by evening for a quiet cottage stay away from crowded city noise.

  • Visit Tehri Lake and Tehri Dam for panoramic mountain and water views.
  • Add water sports when weather and local operators permit.
  • Keep time for peaceful nearby drives, temples, and sunset viewpoints.

About Tehri Lake

Tehri Lake is one of India's largest man-made lakes and one of the most beautiful destinations in Uttarakhand. Formed by the Tehri Dam on the Bhagirathi River, the lake is surrounded by majestic mountains, lush greenery, and spectacular panoramic views.

The lake has become a popular destination for tourists seeking both adventure and relaxation. Visitors can enjoy boating, jet skiing, kayaking, banana rides, water sports, and breathtaking sunset views.

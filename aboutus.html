Explore more about us

At Tehri Lake View, guests can wake up to breathtaking views of the majestic Tehri Lake and surrounding hills. The serene environment, fresh mountain air, and peaceful atmosphere make it an ideal destination for nature lovers and travelers seeking relaxation away from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Our rooms are thoughtfully designed with modern amenities to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable stay. Each room is equipped with comfortable bedding, clean interiors, attached bathrooms, and essential facilities to make your visit convenient and memorable.

Guests can explore nearby attractions, enjoy boating activities at Tehri Lake, take scenic walks through the beautiful landscapes, or simply relax and admire the stunning views from the property. The location offers the perfect balance of adventure and tranquility for visitors of all ages.

We take pride in delivering personalized service and warm hospitality to every guest. Our dedicated staff is always available to assist with travel guidance, local sightseeing recommendations, and special requests to ensure a pleasant experience throughout your stay.

Whether you are visiting for leisure, a family gathering, a romantic getaway, or a business retreat, Tehri Lake View provides the perfect setting for creating unforgettable memories. We look forward to welcoming you and making your stay truly special.

Experience the beauty of Uttarakhand, savor delicious local cuisine, and immerse yourself in the peaceful charm of Tehri. With comfortable accommodations, excellent service, and spectacular lake views, Tehri Lake View is your home away from home.
